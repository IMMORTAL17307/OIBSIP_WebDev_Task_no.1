Our Story

Chai & Chat started with a simple vision: to bring the warmth of a local roadside tea stall (tapri) into a cozy, clean cafe environment. We believe that chai is not just a drink, but an emotion that brings people together.

Whether you want to catch up with friends over a hot cup of tea, read your favorite book, or grab your morning filter coffee, our doors are always open to welcome you.
